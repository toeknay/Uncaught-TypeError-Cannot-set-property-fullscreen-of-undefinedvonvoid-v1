FREQUENTLY ASKED

Questions? We’re Here!

We’re here to answer any questions you have! See our most common questions below, if you don’t see an answer to your question, reach out to our team directly.

Will Spiffy work with my merchant account?

Currently Spiffy order forms will only work with Stripe, however, we have plans to add other gateways soon!

Is the price of Spiffy going to change?

Yes, BUT… if you create an account while we’re still in beta and keep your subscription active you’ll be grandfathered into that pricing for life!

Does Spiffy work with my CRM or marketing software?

We directly integrate with ActiveCampaign and Zapier so as long as your software supports Zapier everything should work perfectly! Check the product roadmap for more integrations on the way!

Does Spiffy charge any transaction fees?

NO WAY! The only tranaction fees you’ll see will come from your merchant account (Stripe).
